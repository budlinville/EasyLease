What is EasyLease?

EasyLease is an online marketplace to connect KU students looking for rental housing and local property owners looking to lease to KU students. The platform allows property owners to post listings of their properties, with information such as address, price, size, and monthly rent, as well as upload pictures. Students looking for rental housing can then search EasyLease for their future home, with filtering available for the number of bedrooms, number of bathrooms, and monthly rent price.



Who are we?

We are a team of EECS students at The University of Kansas currently enrolled in EECS 448, a Software Engineering course. We developed EasyLease for a class project after experiencing first-hand the difficulties of trying to find rental housing as KU students.
